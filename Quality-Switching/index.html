Demographic Survey

Instruction: Thank you for participating in the game streaming survey campaign. A survey study conducted to determine the game streaming QoE based on stall events and quality switching. We have designed three campaigns: Quality Switching vs. Stall Events QvS, Short Stalls vs. Long Stalls (SSvsLS), and Quality Switching (QS). The first step is to watch the stream for thirty to sixty seconds, and second step is to answer 3-4 questions related to the video streamed.
